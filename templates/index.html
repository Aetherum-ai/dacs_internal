(Last Data Update: September 3 2025) Instructions:

To use the DACS simulator, input all the required entries:

  • Credit Score ranges from 300 - 850
  • Assets Under Management (AUM) ranges from 0 - 1,000,000
  • Age ranges from 21 - 75
  • Total Crypto Holdings must be less than or equal to the Assets under management
  • For each crypto coin, select the checkbox and input the percentage of the total crypto holdings that it represents. Please make sure that the total percentages sum up to 100. The website has not been made to handle errors appropriately.
  • For other assets, select the checkbox and input the amount of the assets. Make sure that the sum of the other assets sum up to (Assets Under Management - Total Crypto Holdings).
  • You can also adjust the weights of the different components of the DACS to see how different weights affect the DACS
  • To see how detailed breakdowns of each component, go to the different links on the navigation bar

Disclaimer: This tool is for simulation purposes only and may contain errors. Results are estimates and for guidance only.
